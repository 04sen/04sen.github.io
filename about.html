About Travel Japan

Travel Japan is a passion project turned travel companion—built to help you experience Japan with confidence, curiosity, and respect. We tell the stories behind the places you’ll visit and give you practical tips to make every moment count.

How it started

We began as a photo diary—capturing early-morning shrine visits, empty temple paths, and the glow of Tokyo’s alleys after rain. Those snapshots grew into itineraries, guides for friends, and eventually, this website and a picture book. Along the way we learned that the best trips to Japan balance wonder with good etiquette and a little planning.

What we do

  • Publish approachable guides to shrines, neighborhoods, and seasonal highlights.
  • Share practical advice on when to go, how to move around, and what to look for.
  • Celebrate everyday details—from tea gardens to train station bento.

Our philosophy

We believe travel should be respectful, unhurried, and deeply curious. Whether you’re exploring a quiet temple in Kyoto or a late-night lane in Shinjuku, we aim to help you notice more and stress less.

Milestones

  • 2019 — First photographs collected into a personal travel journal.
  • 2022 — Guides for friends become public articles.
  • 2024 — Picture book published: The Beauty of Japan.
  • 2025 — Travel Japan site launched with curated articles and tools.

About Me 

Born and raised on the beautiful Islands of the Republic of Fiji. Mr. Sachinandan Sen has been part of many Arena's, from advocating for Policy based Advocacy in the Social Services Arena to Fighting in the ring for a gold medal under the National Karate Championship Arena. Its safe to say, Mr. Sen has a diverse set of interests, most prominent of them include golf, practicing martial arts  and photography.

Mr. Sen spent much of his late High School Years volunteering with the United Nations Association of Fiji (UNA Fiji). He served in UNA Fiji for a total of 4 years (Dec 2017– Jul 2022) with 2 years as an Executive Council Member (Jul 2018 - Dec 2020) making important decisions and assisting in writing the NGO's constitution during his tenure . Though no longer directly associated with UNA Fiji, Mr. Sen remains in good relation with Members and Executives and tends to help out in projects and activities whenever possible.

Apart from his volunteer work, Mr. Sen has actively participated with Karate Fiji since the age of Nine! Taking a short break during the Years of 2020 till 2022 due to the Global Pandemic. Till date Mr. Sen has won 2 national gold, 5 national silver, 7 national bronze and 1 international bronze medal. Currently holding a 3rd Kyu (Brown Belt), Mr. Sen hopes to gain his First Black Belt in the coming Years. 

Mr. Sen is currently studying for a Bachelor's Degree in Networking and Security under the University of South Pacific. Even in his University work Mr. Sen took efforts to diverse his portfolio by taking part in the Pacific Hackathon 2022, Finishing 5th in the Pacific. Mr. Sen is looking forward to graduating and is interested to work with technology with a preference towards the cloud computing industry.
